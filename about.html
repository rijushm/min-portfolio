About

My name is Riju Sharma, from Kolkata. I love photography, traveling, reading and everything that’s related to design and technology, completed my schooling and Bachelor’s degree at Kolkata. I studied Electrical Engineering from Techno India and passed out in 2021. During final year I took many courses to spend my lockdown time including Mobile App and Website Development courses. Completed a freelancing project for a USA delivery company which was a Mobile App Development.

Now I am very passionate about creative and unique ideas for web design and development, my expertise ranges from online identity to custom eCommerce websites, web apps for business for professionals to well‐optimized content management systems.

I have got an experience of more than 2.5 years in the design field and contineously working to give my best to every client’s or brand’s needs.

The design, development and management of your website is my daily work. My mission is to expand the potential of online existence. I do some cool stuff like javascript animation, lovable interactions, or managing and analyzing website traffic. When I am designing the website of any brand, I begin with the goal of a target user and think about how to make their work easier.

‟Saweet and Interactive” websites that will be loved by users is my only Philosophy.
